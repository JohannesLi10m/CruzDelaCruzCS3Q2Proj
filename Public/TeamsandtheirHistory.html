Scuderia Ferrari HP

Ferrari, F1's oldest and most competitive team, they have the record amount of 15 Constructor's and 16 Driver's Championships won. They started in 1950, they were supposed to be racing cars made by Alfa Romeo, but instead chose to build their cars for the sport, though having an unsuccessful first season in F1, they did win their first Grand Prix in 1951 at the British Grand Prix(Silverstone).
