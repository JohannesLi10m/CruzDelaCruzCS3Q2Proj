2026 F1 Drivers

F1 Drivers are those who sit behind the wheel of a car that reaches 300 km/h, these people must be physically trained, they fight to gain positions, these positions help them score points for their Championship fight. Without drivers, F1 wouldn't be F1, with no one controlling the cars, then there is no race, no championship, no on-track battles that we watch for entertainment. These guys are some of the best in motorsports.
