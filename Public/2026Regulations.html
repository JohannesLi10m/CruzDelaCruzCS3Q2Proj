2026 Car Regulations:

The new and improved car regulation for the 2026 season onwards includes a lot of changes for the cars to become more eco-friendly, faster, safer, and more importantly, better racing. The weight of the cars will be lessened by 30 kg, a power unit which will be more eco-friendly as it will use sustainable gasses without changing the performance of the car, active aero will make its return to the sport as X mode(Faster) and Z mode(Normal). Manual Override Mode(MOM) will be a replacement for the Overtake Battery as drivers can activate it at anytime they want to gain on their opponents and overtake them, and more importantly, the safety of the cars will be better as the safety equipment will be tested with harder and harder tests to determine if they are race safe.
