Legendary Drivers of F1

These Drivers are the ones who have been an icon to the sport by winning Championships in style. They have shown their supreme skill and amazing way of driving the car to its limits.

Juan Manuel Fangio, 5xWDC(1951, 1954-57)

Jim Clark, 2xWDC(1963, 1965)

Niki Lauda, 3xWDC(1975, 1977, 1984)

James Hunt, 1xWDC(1976)

Alain Prost, 4xWDC(1985-86, 1989, 1993)

Ayrton Senna, 3xWDC(1988, 1990-91)

Michael Schumacher, 7xWDC(1994-95, 2000-04)

Mika Hakkinen, 2xWDC(1998-99)

Fernando Alonso, 2xWDC(2005-06)

Kimi Raikkonen, 1xWDC(2007)

Lewis Hamilton, 7x(2008, 2014-15, 2017-20)

Jenson Button, 1x(2009)

Sebastian Vettel, 4xWDC(2010-13)

Max Verstappen, 4xWDC(2021-24)
